About

COVIDSCAPE

COVIDSCAPE 2020 all rights reserved.
Due to COVID-19, we are facing risks whenever we are outside and near other people. However, it is hard to calculate the amount of risk we are exposing ourselves to by being at a location for a certain amount of time. COVIDSCAPE seeks to answer this problem by not only being able to detect and analyze current and past risk levels but even predict potential risk before user goes to the desired location.
We quantify and predict risks of COVID-19 by feeding client data containing a history of location and other relevant data to an algorithm using a set of risk factors generated from valid data sources. Location, movement and other data are collected from users using sensors in mobile devices.
In addition, COVIDSCAPE will be able to back-trace clients who have tested positive and notify other clients who may have contacted with the diagnosed client.
